## Description
Budget Planner helps you understand your money in minutes.

Track daily expenses, set category budgets, and instantly view how much you have spent this month. The Insights screen highlights your biggest spending areas and recommends practical ways to save.

Built for fast daily use, Budget Planner keeps your data private on your device, with optional iCloud sync across your Apple devices.

### Key Features
- Add and organize expenses by category
- Set monthly budgets for each category
- View current-month spending summary
- Get savings opportunities based on your spending pattern
- Create recurring expenses (weekly/monthly)
- Set daily local reminders to review your budget
- Export monthly reports as CSV and PDF
- Optional iCloud sync support

### Great For
- Personal monthly budgeting
- Families tracking household costs
- Students managing fixed allowances
- Anyone reducing unnecessary spending

Budget Planner is designed as a practical, no-noise money tracker so you can stay consistent and improve month by month.
