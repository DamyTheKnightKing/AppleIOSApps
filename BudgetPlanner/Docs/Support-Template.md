## Common Questions

### 1. My data is missing after reinstall.
If iCloud sync was disabled, local app data may be removed when app is deleted.

### 2. How do recurring expenses work?
Recurring expenses create entries automatically based on weekly/monthly schedules.

### 3. Why am I not getting reminders?
Check iOS notification permission in Settings and confirm reminder is enabled in app settings.

### 4. How can I export my spending?
Go to Insights and tap Export CSV or Export PDF, then share/save the file.

### 5. How do I report a bug?
Send app version, iPhone model, iOS version, and issue steps to support email.
